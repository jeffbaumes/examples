ENCODE Genome Data

Note: Data may take 30 seconds or so to load.

Human gene annotation data from the ENCODE project. Each of 16,000 annotations are displayed in linear order, grouped by chromosome. Blue indicated that the annotation is encoded on the forward strand, while orange annotations are on the reverse strand. Color intensity shows the length of each annoation, with the strongest colors having 10,000 or more base pairs.

Source: ENCODE Genes V12
