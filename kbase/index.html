Microbe Community

Note: Works best in the latest versions of Chrome and Safari.

This visualization displays a microbial community from the KBase project. On the left is a spatial depiction of the microbes (fabricated), while the right shows a pseudo-spatial organization of the microbes aggregated by groups at a certain taxonomic level. Each dot represents ~10,000 in abundance.

Coloring by model completion shows (fabricated) data on what percent of the organism's processes are currently modeled.
